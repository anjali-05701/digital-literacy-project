Responsible Social Media Use – Do’s and Don’ts

✔ Do’s

1.Maintain a professional and respectful tone while posting online.

2.Protect your personal information and privacy settings.

3.Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.

4.Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

5.Use social media to build a positive digital presence.

❌ Don’ts

1.Do not share offensive, abusive, or harmful content.

2.Do not post confidential academic or institutional information.

3.Avoid engaging in online arguments or cyberbullying.

4.Do not click unknown links or suspicious messages.

5.Avoid posting content that could harm your professional reputation.
