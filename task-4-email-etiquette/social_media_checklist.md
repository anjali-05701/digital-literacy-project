Responsible Social Media Use – Do’s and Don’ts

✔ Do’s

1.Keep a professional and respectful tone while posting anything online.

2.Keep your personal details and privacy settings safe.

3.Verify the authenticity of the content before posting to avoid the spread of false information.

4.Keep strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

5.Use social media to develop a positive online presence.

❌ Don’ts

1.Do not post offensive, abusive, and harmful content.

2.Do not post confidential academic and institutional-related content.

3.Avoid getting into arguments and cyberbullying.

4.Do not click on any link or message from unknown senders.

5.Avoid posting anything that might harm your professional reputation.
