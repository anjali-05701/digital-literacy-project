Task 4 – Email Etiquette & Social Media Guidelines
Objective

The objective of this task was to understand the importance of professional digital communication and responsible behavior on online platforms. As students increasingly communicate through digital channels, maintaining proper email etiquette and social media discipline is essential.

Professional Email Drafts

Two professional email drafts were created to demonstrate correct email etiquette:

Email to a Professor – Requesting an extension for an assignment submission deadline in a respectful and formal manner.
Email to an Internship Coordinator – Expressing interest in internship opportunities and seeking information about application procedures.

Both emails include a clear subject line, professional greeting, structured message body, and a polite closing signature.

Social Media Etiquette Checklist

A checklist containing Do’s and Don’ts for responsible social media usage was also created. This checklist highlights the importance of maintaining a positive digital identity, protecting personal information, and avoiding harmful online behavior.

Learning Outcome

This task helped me understand that digital communication should always be respectful, clear, and professional. I learned how a well-written email can create a positive impression in academic and professional environments. I also realized that responsible social media behavior is important because our online actions can influence our reputation and future opportunities.
