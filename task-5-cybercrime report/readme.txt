Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study
Objective

The objective of this task is to understand common cybercrimes and create awareness about how individuals can protect themselves while using digital technologies. As digital platforms are widely used for communication, payments, and learning, it is important to recognize potential online threats and take preventive measures.

Case Study Overview

For this task, a case study on UPI Online Payment Fraud was prepared. This type of cybercrime occurs when attackers manipulate victims into approving fraudulent payment requests or sharing confidential financial information such as OTP or UPI PIN.

The case study explains how cybercriminals typically carry out such frauds, who the common targets are, and the possible consequences such as financial loss, misuse of personal information, and emotional stress.

Prevention Checklist

A prevention checklist was also created to help students understand safe online practices. The checklist includes guidelines for protecting personal information, using secure passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and following safe digital payment practices. Special emphasis was placed on financial safety while using UPI payment applications.

The checklist also provides official reporting channels for cybercrime incidents in India, including the National Cyber Crime Portal and the cybercrime helpline 1930.

Learning Outcome

Through this task, I learned how cybercriminals exploit lack of awareness and trust to carry out online frauds. This activity helped me understand the importance of digital hygiene, secure online behavior, and reporting suspicious activities promptly. Developing awareness about cyber threats is essential for students so they can protect their personal and financial information in the digital environment.
