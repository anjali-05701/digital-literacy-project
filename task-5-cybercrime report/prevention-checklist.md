Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

--General Online Safety Tips

Always verify the identity of unknown callers claiming to represent banks or companies.
Do not click suspicious links received through SMS, email, or social media.
Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible.
Install applications only from official app stores and trusted sources.
Keep your device software and security updates up to date.
Financial and UPI Safety Tips
Never share OTP, ATM PIN, UPI PIN, or banking passwords with anyone.
Remember that scanning a QR code sends money; it does not receive money.
Never approve unknown payment requests on your UPI app.
Reporting Cybercrime in India

If you become a victim of cybercrime, immediately report it through the National Cyber Crime Portal.

Website: https://cybercrime.gov.in

You can also call the Cybercrime Helpline: 1930 for immediate assistance.

Reporting cybercrime quickly increases the chances of recovering lost funds and helps authorities track cybercriminal activities.
